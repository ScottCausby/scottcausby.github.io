Project 2
Digital Scratch Map

We used Ruby on rails, HTML/CSS, JavaScript, React and a Bcrypt gem. The purpose of the app is to simulate a scratch-map of the world where users can log in, keep track of the countries they've visited and make a bucket list of the countries they wish to visit. It's a simple app based on three models; Users, Countries and Visits.
